  1. Above, Staten Island’s Rooftop Wedding Venue

Above is a sophisticated rooftop-wedding venue connected to the Hilton Garden Inn, Staten Island. The rooftop features an open air wraparound terrace with panoramic views of the New York City skyline along with cabanas, fire pits, an open kitchen, and a modern cocktail hour and after party with a dramatic 50-foot-long Calacatta marble illuminated bar. One floor below, guests will experience modern seasonal American cuisine in a stunning metropolitan ballroom with 20-foot floor-to-ceiling windows, a single-slab Carrera marble candle wall, Venetian glass infused wallpaper and a dramatic light fixture with 4,000 Swarovski crystal LED lights. Above towers 10 stories atop stylish hotel rooms and suites; perfect for the honeymoon couple, bridesmaids and guests.

    2. Reception area

  1. Van Cortlandt Golf Lake House-

Lakefront historic venue 20min from Manhattan. The Lake House at Van Cortlandt Park Golf Course sets the stage for a very special occasion with a lake front view and one of the most historic Event Venues in New York. If you are looking for your next event location, you don’t have to look any further. Whether your event is an intimate cocktail reception or a formal affair, we will make sure that our space exceeds your expectations. The Lake House is located in the North West corner of the Bronx, only several short miles from the border with Westchester County. The Lake House is so named because of its proximity to Van Cortlandt Lake. It has a 2,500+ sq. ft. outdoor deck with lovely views of the lake. Of course both the Lake House and the lake are located in Van Cortlandt Park.
